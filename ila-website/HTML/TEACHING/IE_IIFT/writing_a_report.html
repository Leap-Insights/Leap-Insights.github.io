Information regarding the term paper

Guidelines for writing the term paper

Listed below are the guidelines for organising the term paper
  • Page 1 should contain:
    1. Title
    2. Author
    3. Abstract: 100 words
  • The rest of the paper should be organised as:
    1. Executive summary of the paper (300 words)
    2. Background/Motivation (200 words)
    3. Questions asked in the paper (50-80 words)
    4. Evidence and answers (1200-1500 words)
    5. Conclusion (100 words)

Other instructions

  • A print out of the report is to be submitted on the day of the presentation. A submission by email, without a printout, will be considered a non submission
  • Each presentation should be of 20 minutes
  • The reward for the three best reports will be a dinner and the reports will be put up on this website :-)
