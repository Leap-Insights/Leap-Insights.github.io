Internships

At Leap Insights, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of thought leaders and change-makers. Our research internship program offers a unique opportunity for students and recent graduates to gain invaluable hands-on experience in the fields of economics, law, and policy. Through this immersive program, interns will have the chance to work alongside our team of experts, contributing to groundbreaking research projects that shape policies and drive positive change.

Our flexible internship program allows you to participate either as a volunteer or through your university's course offerings. For those seeking academic credit, we collaborate with numerous institutions to ensure our internships qualify as a course, seamlessly integrating into your curriculum. Alternatively, if you prefer a volunteer-based experience, we welcome your contribution and commitment to our research initiatives. To apply, please fill out the Google form on the right.

At Leap Insights, we believe in fostering an environment of collaboration, innovation, and intellectual curiosity. Interns will have the opportunity to participate in interdisciplinary discussions, attend workshops and seminars, and receive mentorship from industry professionals. Our goal is to equip you with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to navigate the complex landscapes of economics, law, and policy, empowering you to make a lasting impact in your chosen field.
