Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information through our Digital Presence Survey Questionnaire, which includes:

  • Basic business information (e.g., type of business, years of operation, number of employees, revenue)
  • Current digital presence (e.g., website, social media platforms, payment methods)
  • Digital operations (e.g., communication methods, online advertising)
  • Challenges and needs in adopting digital tools
  • Impact and future plans regarding digital tools
  • Demographic information (optional)

How We Use Your Information

The information collected through the survey will be used to understand the digital presence and needs of businesses. This data helps us provide better support and resources to businesses looking to enhance their digital operations.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is:

  • Encrypted during transmission
  • Stored on secure servers
  • Accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Anonymized in reports and analysis

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. When the data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with research partners and service providers who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our survey platform. These cookies do not track your browsing activity on other websites.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified to users through our website or email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: director@leap-insights.org

By participating in this survey, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described above.
